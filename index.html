Master's Degree (Energy Engineering)

Feasibility of Micro Nuclear Reactors in desalination plants

I studied the possibility of using Micro Modular Reactors (1-20 MW) to power Reverse Osmosis desalination plants, together with renwables sources. I developed an optimization model in Python to determine the optimal design and managment of the energy system, aiming to minimize costs or CO2 emissions.
